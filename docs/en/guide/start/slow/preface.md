# Overview

If there's a "Getting Started", there should also be a "Slow Start".

This section may grow quite long, and I may not finish every piece right away. Still, I'll keep filling it in so people who are new to this kind of tool — or who aren't developers but want to try Hunea (or similar agent products, TUI or otherwise) for productivity or pure curiosity — can onboard more comfortably.

"Slow Start" is not for everyone. If you already use tools like this, or you already have a workflow that works, this series may not be the best use of your time. Of course you're still welcome to browse, and feedback that helps improve the series is always appreciated.
